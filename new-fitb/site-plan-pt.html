Overview

Purpose

My website helps you connect with personal trainers so you can finally cross off something on your New Year’s resolution. The website can give you tips and tricks for healthy eating habits or even give you an idea for a quick exercise. No matter how hopeless you feel, our personal trainers are here to help you in your unique goals.

Audience

Anyone who is wanting to get more active or even want to try something new. It can also be college kids who need recipe ideas. Another audience is personal trainers themselves if they want to connect with others they can.

Content

Home page

This site is for those who is in need of a personal trainer, it gives a list of what type of trainer we can offer. With an option to request a specific trainer that we don’t have, and our team will find someone for you. Whether you are training for a marathon, wanting to try a new sport, want to try Yoga, need life coaching, want to lose weight, or get stronger we have a network of trainers that can help with your goals. People will come to us because we have trainers who are here for you and not just to get paid, in the process they will become your friend. The main page will tell them why they should get a personal trainer. It also will be a way where they can go to the other pages where they can get to know the trainers and where they can get easy recipes. I would want to put something where they can sign up for a trainer and get started on their journey. There will also be pictures and maybe a video that inspires others to get a personal trainer. Lastly, there will be a section where people who already was with a trainer can tell their success story and their experience. This will hopefully motivate others to try a trainer for themselves when they see the reviews that our website has.

Images for the Home page

Meet A Trainer

This page is for the users to get to know the trainers that we offer. It will show a picture of them, what they train in, and a bio of them. This will help gain trust with the user since they know who they will be working with for the next couple days, weeks, or months. It will also show their social media so the user can see more of what they do on their personal page that they can’t see on our page. There could also be a link to their Spotify or Apple Music playlist that motivates them when they train, in hopes of motivating others to accomplish their goals. People want to know if the trainer knows what they are talking about, having the bios let others know their story. This builds the trainers credibility for them to train the person in need. This can also inspire others to also become a part of the network of trainers. Because of this I will have a button where they can click to become a trainer. I will have another button where others can refer a friend to be a personal trainer or to receive a personal trainer.

Images for Meet A Trainer

Recipes

This section of the website is for those people who needs a heather option for their meals. It is also for college students who is sick and tired of eating mac and cheese and ramen noodles all the time. It could also be for frantic moms who wants to feed her army of children without them complaining. It will help people be more creative and give them a sense of fulfillment when they make something from our website. Who doesn’t like eating something they make that was cheap, easy, and delicious. It will also have a spot where people can put their family recipes where they can share what food brings them joy. Of course, there will be pictures to motivate the person to make the food that our website offers. There can also be a spot where people can put a review of the recipe they tried. People will come to this site since we are offering this service for free because we know the struggle of knowing what to make.
